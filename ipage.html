"Black Panther"- title held by T’Challa, a member of the royal family of the fictional African country of Wakanda, given after the death of his father. T’Challa claimed the throne and the role of Black Panther in which he was exposed to a mystical herb that enhanced his strength and agility to near-superhuman levels. As the king of the African nation of Wakanda, T’Challa protects his people as the latest in a legacy line of Black Panther warriors.

Hand-to-hand Combat

A terrific hand-to-hand combatant, T’Challa is highly skilled in one-on-one fighting, even without the powers of the Black Panther.

Bulletproof Costume

Black Panther’s bulletproof costume, designed by his sister Shuri, is stored within his necklace and can envelop T’Challa whenever he wishes.

Vibranium Claws

The Black Panther costume includes very tough and sharp vibranium claws, which can retract back into the gloves.

Energy Pulse

The Black Panther costume absorbs kinetic energy, and then redistributes it, allowing T’Challa to either increase the strength of his kicks and punches, or release all the energy at once in the form of a powerful energy pulse.
