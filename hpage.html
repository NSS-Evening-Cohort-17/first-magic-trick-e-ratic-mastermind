About

Notably powerful, Wanda Maximoff (aka Scarlet Witch) has fought both against and with the Avengers, attempting to hone her abilities and do what she believes is right to help the world. Maximoff is depicted as a Sokovian refugee who, along with her twin brother Pietro, volunteers to be experimented on by Hydra. The Mind Stone amplifies her natural telekinetic and energy manipulation abilities known as Chaos Magic, and she eventually taps into her latent powers and assumes the ancient title of the Scarlet Witch. Maximoff initially comes into conflict with the Avengers but later joins them and becomes one of the most powerful members.

Appears in: Captain America: The Winter Soldier, Avengers: Age of Ultron, Captain America: Civil War, Avengers: Infinity War, Avengers: Endgame, WandaVision, and Doctor Strange: Multiverse of Madness.
